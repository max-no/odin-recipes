Kraft Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

A quick and easy dinner made with creamy cheese sauce and tender macaroni. Perfect for busy weeknights or a comforting meal anytime.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Steps

  1. Boil 4 cups of water in a medium-sized pot.
  2. Add the macaroni from the box to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the macaroni for 7-8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Drain the macaroni and return it to the pot.
  5. Add 1/4 cup of milk, 2 tablespoons of butter, and the cheese powder from the box to the macaroni.
  6. Stir everything together until the cheese sauce is smooth and evenly coats the macaroni.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
