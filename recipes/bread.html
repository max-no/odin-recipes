Simple Bread Recipe

Description

A new take on a classic bread, now with an overnight rest for deeper flavor and a lighter, airier texture. The slow rise brings out rich aromas and a subtle tang that quick rise breads can’t match.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • Extra flour for dusting

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and yeast.
  2. Add the water and mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 12-18 hours.
  4. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Place a Dutch oven or heavy pot with a lid inside to heat up.
  5. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and shape it into a ball.
  6. Carefully remove the hot pot from the oven and place the dough inside.
  7. Cover with the lid and bake for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove the lid and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown.
  9. Let the bread cool on a wire rack before slicing and serving.
